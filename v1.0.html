myWorldLink Connection Guide

For Android Users

  • Go to Wi-Fi Settings and select the SSID "myWorldLink".
  • If available, click on "Advanced Options".
  • Select "PEAP" in the EAP method.
  • Select "MSCHAPV2" in Phase 2 authentication.
  • Choose "Do not validate" or "Unspecified" in the CA certificate field.
  • Type your WorldLink account username in the Identity field.
  • In the Password section, enter your WorldLink Online Service password.
  • Click "CONNECT".

For iPhone/Windows/PC Users

  • Go to Settings and select Wi-Fi.
  • Select the SSID "myWorldLink" from the list.
  • Enter your WorldLink account Username & Password.
  • Click "CONNECT".

For Non-WorldLink Customers

  • Select the SSID "Free_WorldLink_Wi-Fi".
  • Click on "Get Online".
  • Click on "Connect on Free Wi-Fi".
  • Click on "Are you a new user?".
  • Insert your mobile number and click "Next".
  • Confirm your mobile number and click "Continue".
  • Enter the OTP received via SMS and click "Login".
